Research Insights

Acoustic Features We extract MFCCs, formant frequencies, and temporal rhythms proven to correlate with hunger vs. discomfort cries.
Machine Learning Our ensemble of CNN and SVM models achieves >92% accuracy on labeled audio datasets (Zhang et al., 2023).
Real-Time Feedback Low-latency processing (<200ms) ensures you get the “reason” almost instantly after the first cry.
Clinical Relevance Early detection can flag potential health issues (e.g. colic, reflux) for pediatric follow-up.
