Tutorial

Follow these steps to use the Infant Cry Detection system effectively:

Step 1: Preparation

Ensure that you record in a quiet environment and have your device ready.

Step 2: Upload

Navigate to the Cry Detection page and upload your audio file using the form.

Step 3: Analysis

Our SVM model analyzes the cry and categorizes it appropriately to ensure the best result.

Step 4: Results

View the outcome and follow the recommendations provided by the system.

Tips

Follow these tips to improve the accuracy of the detection system:

Optimal Recording

Use a quiet environment to avoid background noise and improve detection accuracy.

File Format

Use standard audio formats such as .mp3 or .wav to ensure compatibility with the system.

Recording Duration

Record at least 5-10 seconds of continuous audio for the best analysis results.

Microphone Settings

Ensure your device's microphone is properly configured to capture clear audio.
