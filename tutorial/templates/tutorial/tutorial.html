Pro Tips

Maximize accuracy with these expert recommendations

Optimal Recording Quality

Record in a quiet environment to minimize background noise. Clear audio improves our 99.48% accuracy rate even further.

Best Audio Formats

Use WAV format for highest accuracy (lossless), followed by MP3. Avoid heavily compressed formats for better results.

Ideal Recording Duration

Capture 5-15 seconds of continuous crying for optimal analysis. This provides enough data for our feature extraction algorithms.

Microphone Best Practices

Position your device 1-2 feet from the baby. Ensure microphone permissions are enabled and volume levels are adequate.
