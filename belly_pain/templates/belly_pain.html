Belly Pain in Babies

1. Crying more than usual

Reason: Gas

Treatment: Bicycle baby's legs

See a doctor if: Fever of 100.4°F (38°C) or above

2. Spitting up or vomiting

Reason: Constipation

Treatment: Take a look at their diet

See a doctor if: Diarrhea that lasts more than 48 hours

3. Refusing to eat

Reason: Formula ingredients

Treatment: Take a look at your diet

See a doctor if: Constipation that lasts more than a few days

4. Unusual sleep disruptions

Reason: Allergies or sensitivities

Treatment: Address feeding issues

See a doctor if: Signs of extreme stress like nonstop screaming

5. Diarrhea or constipation

Reason: GERD (reflux)

Treatment: Consider a different formula

See a doctor if: Extreme fatigue

6. Making grimacing faces

Reason: Breast milk

Treatment: Get things moving

See a doctor if: A rigid belly

7. Tense body language

Reason: Medication

Treatment: Switch up burping strategy

See a doctor if: Weight loss

8. Bilious

Reason: –

Treatment: –

See a doctor if: Vomiting blood or black, tarry stool
