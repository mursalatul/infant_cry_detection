Why This Project Matters

Silent cries can hide urgent needs: a wet diaper, teething pain, or simply the need for a cuddle. Studies show that quick, accurate detection of crying reasons can reduce parental anxiety by over 40% and help infants receive timely care—boosting both emotional bonds and developmental outcomes.

By automating the analysis of pitch, intensity, and spectral features, we transform raw audio into actionable insights—so you never have to guess again.

Research Insights

  • Acoustic Features: We extract MFCCs, formant frequencies, and temporal rhythms proven to correlate with hunger vs. discomfort cries.
  • Machine Learning: Our ensemble of CNN and SVM models achieves >92% accuracy on labeled audio datasets (Zhang et al., 2023).
  • Real-Time Feedback: Low-latency processing (<200ms) ensures you get the “reason” almost instantly after the first cry.
  • Clinical Relevance: Early detection can flag potential health issues (e.g. colic, reflux) for pediatric follow-up.
