Tutorial & Tips

Tutorial

Follow these steps to use the Infant Cry Detection system:

  1. Preparation: Ensure that you record in a quiet environment and have your device ready.
  2. Upload: Navigate to the Cry Detection page and upload your audio file using the form.
  3. Analysis: Our SVM model then analyzes the cry and categorizes it as hungry, tired, needing burping, experiencing belly pain, or indicating discomfort.
  4. Results: View the outcome and follow the recommendations provided by the system.

Tips

  • Optimal Recording: Use a quiet environment to avoid background noise.
  • Consistent Distance: Keep a consistent distance between your baby and the recording device.
  • Duration: Aim for a recording of at least 5-10 seconds of continuous audio.

  • Real-Time Feedback: Consider recording multiple samples to compare results if needed.
