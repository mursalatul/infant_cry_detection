Tutorial & Tips

Learn how to get the most out of our Infant Cry Detection system

Tutorial

Step 1: Preparation

Ensure that you record in a quiet environment and have your device ready.

Step 2: Upload

Navigate to the Cry Detection page and upload your audio file using the form.

Step 3: Analysis

Our SVM model then analyzes the cry and categorizes it as hungry, tired, needing burping, belly pain, or indicating discomfort.

Step 4: Results

View the outcome and follow the recommendations provided by the system. For further assistance, consult our FAQ or contact support.

Tips

  • Optimal Recording: Use a quiet environment to avoid background noise.
  • Consistent Distance: Keep a consistent distance between your baby and the recording device.
  • File Format: Use standard audio formats such as .mp3 or .wav to ensure compatibility.
  • Duration: Aim for a recording of at least 5-10 seconds of continuous audio.
  • Microphone Settings: Ensure your device's microphone is configured to capture clear audio.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Consider recording multiple samples to compare results if needed.

Implementing these tips can help improve the accuracy and reliability of the detection system.
